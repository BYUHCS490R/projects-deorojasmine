Engagement Answers: November 24, 2025

1. What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube? Positives: YouTube hosting is free, fast, and easy to embed into your HTML page with just one iframe. Negatives: You don't control the ads, recommended videos, or distractions that YouTube shows around your content.

2. What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform? The most useful thing a CSS transform does is let you move, rotate, scale, or skew an element without changing the actual layout of your HTML page.

3. What is the schedule for the rest of the class? The schedule is basically the final project work period, where you build your multi-page website, finish your form processing, and submit the final project at the end of the semester.
