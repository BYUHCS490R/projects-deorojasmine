Background Images

1. When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag? Use a background image when the picture is decorative or part of the pages design, like a banner, texture, or pattern behind text. Use the img tag when the image is part of the pages content and needs to be seen or described (for example, a photo, logo, or diagram) because it can include alt text for accessibility.
