Engagement Answers Today: November 07, 2025

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? The "action" attribute tells the browser where to send the form data when it's submitted.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? The "method" attribute specifies how the form data will be sent to the server.

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"? "GET" sends data through the URL (visible), while "POST" sends data securely in the request body (hidden).
