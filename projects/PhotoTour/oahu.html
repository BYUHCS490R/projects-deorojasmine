Oahu

Oahu, known as "The Gathering Place," is the most populous island in Hawaii and home to the state capital, Honolulu. It offers a mix of urban and natural attractions, including the famous Waikiki Beach, Pearl Harbor, and the scenic North Shore, known for its big wave surfing. Oahu also has lush valleys, hiking trails, and cultural sites that showcase Hawaiian history and traditions.
