The Hawaiian Islands is home to the world's best islands, beaches, and marine wildlife. Visitors to the Hawaiian Island can experience unique natural wonders such as black sand beaches, waterfalls, and the famous Mauna Kea observatory. The island's vibrant local culture, delicious cuisine, and welcoming communities make it an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking adventure and relaxation. I chose these pictures and places because I am currently living in Hawaii as an international student. One of my goals is to visit all the Hawaiian islands before I graduate and leave Hawaii. So far the only remaining island I have not visited is Lanai and Molokai.
