Big Island

Hawaii, the biggest Island in Hawaii, is known for its diverse landscapes, including active volcanoes, lush rainforests, and beautiful beaches. It's a popular destination for outdoor activities like hiking, snorkeling, and exploring volcanic craters. The island also has rich cultural heritage sites and offers stunning views of the Pacific Ocean.
