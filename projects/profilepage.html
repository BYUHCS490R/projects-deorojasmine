Hi, my name is Jasmine Rio De Oro

I am currently a senior Accounting & Business Major student. I have been in the island of Oahu for more than three years. I am grateful that I decided to pursue my education here becuase of the great opportunities we have in school. The Brigham Young University-Hawaii is a wonderful place to "Enter Forth to Learn and Go Forth to Serve" which is primarily the school's motto. Some of the things I have been grateful for the past years are the following:

  1. The opportunity to serve in the House of the Lord as a temple worker.
  2. The blessing of being a full-time student as I pursue my education while working as a part-time Tour Guide in the Polynesian Cultural Center.
  3. The people I meet not just from where I came from the Philippines but all the world who become part of my Ohana.

I am excited for the next chapter of my life especially graduation is in the next two semesters. Sometimes I become a bit anxious about what to do next. Whenever I felt the pressure I strive to remember that aligning the things I wanted to what Heavenly Father's wants me to do. I go to the temple and seek spiritual guidance, constantly prioritizing Him. I love one of the famous qoute I live by from,

 President Ezra Taft Benson
