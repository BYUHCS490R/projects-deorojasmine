2. Content on the Home Page and Five Content Pages

My website will have a home page and five additional content pages. Each page will contain original text, photos, and information that I create myself.

  • Home Page: Welcome message, brief intro about me, overview of the website, small previews of the destination pages, and navigation links.
  • Page 1: My background as a traveler, how I moved from the Philippines to Hawaii, all the places I have visited, and how travel influences my life.
  • Page 2 (Table Page): A table listing each destination I have visited (Fiji, Mexico, Utah, Washington, California, Nevada, and Hawaii) plus my dream destination (Europe). Table includes columns for destination, type (country/state/island), visited/dream, highlights, and notes.
  • Page 3: A page explaining how I plan trips as a student, including budgeting, saving money, sample expenses, and general travel safety tips.
  • Page 4: A gallery of photos I personally took, including Hawaii beaches, PCC, temple areas, cruise photos, and other places I traveled. Each photo will have a caption explaining the memory.
  • Page 5 (Form Page): A page with an HTML form so visitors can send messages, ask travel questions, or share their favorite travel spots.
